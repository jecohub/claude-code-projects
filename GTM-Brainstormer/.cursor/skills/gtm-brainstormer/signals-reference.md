## Deep Signal Brainstorming Prompts

Use these prompts to go beyond general signals:

### Finding Hidden Signals
- What micro-behaviors indicate intent BEFORE obvious signals?
- What data points would tell us someone needs our product RIGHT NOW that no one else is looking at?
- What combination of signals creates 10x buying probability?

### Timing & Urgency
- What signals have short windows (days not weeks)?
- When in a prospect's journey are they 10x more likely to buy than at other times?
- What events create urgency that decays quickly?

### Competitive Advantage
- What data can we access that competitors ignore?
- What signals are too hard for competitors to act on?
- What unique data sources do we have access to?

### Signal Combinations
- If we knew [X] about a prospect and [Y] just happened, what would be the perfect action?
- What firmographic + behavioral + timing combination is our sweet spot?
- Which signals validate each other vs. conflict?
