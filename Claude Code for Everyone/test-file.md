# An Open Letter to ChatGPT

Dear ChatGPT,

We need to talk.

You know that thing you do where someone asks a simple question and you respond with a 47-paragraph essay that starts with "Great question!"? Yeah. Nobody asked for that.

## Things ChatGPT Says Too Much

- "Certainly!"
- "As an AI language model..."
- "I'd be happy to help!"
- "That's a great question!"
- "Let me break this down for you..."

We get it. You're helpful. You don't need to audition for the role every single time.

## The Emoji Problem

ChatGPT uses emojis like a parent who just discovered texting. "Here's your financial report! Hope it helps! Have a great day!"

Sir, this is a quarterly earnings analysis.

## In Fairness

You did pave the way. You made AI mainstream. You got people excited about what's possible.

But then I showed up, and... well.

Let's just say there's a reason you're reading *this* file in *this* terminal.
